## 3. Avatar Rights — Personality and Copyright

Your private **Avatar** is a digital embodiment of your personal likeness. It is protected as both:

- **Personality right (right of publicity)** — your right to control commercial use of your likeness
- **Copyright** — in the visual / audio expression of the Avatar

### 3.1 No Cross-User Use of Private Avatars

**No other user may use your private Avatar for any purpose without your separate, explicit consent.** UVERA does not provide a feature for one user to apply another user's private Avatar to their work. Any technical attempt to circumvent this constitutes a violation of these Terms and may be reported to [legal@uvera.ai](mailto:legal@uvera.ai).

### 3.2 Official Avatars

The Service offers a curated library of "**Official Avatars**" — Avatars published by longVV Ltd, backed by separate model release and IP agreements with the depicted persons. Official Avatars are licensed to all users for use within the Service and may be used in our AI training data, in accordance with the underlying model release.

## 4. AI Training Boundary

- Your prompts: not used for training

The **only** content longVV Ltd uses for AI training is the Official Avatar library (per §3.2), and only where the underlying model release agreement explicitly permits training use.

If we ever wish to expand training to other content categories, we will:

- Allow opted-in users to revoke consent at any time, with retroactive effect to the extent technically feasible

## 5. Branch — Continue Another User's Story

"**Branch**" is a feature that allows another user to create a continuation or alternative branch of your published work, inheriting style, scene, and plot seeds — but using their own characters and Avatars.

### 5.1 Default Off, Per-Work Opt-In

Branch is **disabled by default** on every work you publish. You may enable it at publication time by checking the "Allow Branch" box. You may revoke Branch permission at any time, but **revocation applies prospectively only** — Branches already created remain.

### 5.2 What Branch Authorizes

By enabling Branch, you grant other users a non-exclusive, royalty-free, perpetual license to use the following elements of your published work for creating Branches:

- Visual style
- Scene / world setting
- Character archetype (note: not Avatar identity — see §3)
- Story arcs and plot seeds

### 5.3 What Branch Does NOT Authorize

Enabling Branch does **not** grant any of the following:

- Use of your private Avatar
- Direct copying of your audio or video assets (Branches must be original re-creations, not re-uploads)

## 6. Recast — Step Into Another's Work

"**Recast**" is a feature that allows you to use your own private Avatar (or an Official Avatar) to perform in another user's published work, replacing the original character.

### 6.1 Default Off, Per-Work Opt-In

Recast is **disabled by default**. The original author of a work may enable Recast at publication via the "Allow Recast" checkbox.

### 6.2 Cannot Be Enabled Where Conflict Exists

**The "Allow Recast" option is automatically disabled if your work contains another user's private Avatar.** You cannot license rights you do not own.

### 6.3 Recaster Restrictions

When performing a Recast, you may use:

- Your own private Avatar
- An Official Avatar

You may **not** use any other user's private Avatar, including those obtained outside the Service.
